assess the piece for
1. Text: What is the medium, what tools do I need to decipher the text, tools were

2. Author - Assess and understand an author's background as foundational to
their text
3. Audience - Assess and understand an audience’s background as foundational
to why and how they will be reading the text
4. Purpose - Establish the rhetorical purpose of BOTH the writer/creator of the
piece, AND the audience
